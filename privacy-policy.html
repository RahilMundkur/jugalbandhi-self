Jugalbandhi Self

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Jugalbandhi Self is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device to preserve your reading experience. This includes your current reading position, bookmarks, personal notes, display preferences (such as font size and dark mode), and your chosen language. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Internet Access

All book content — including all 35 language translations — is embedded within the app and available offline. No internet connection is required to read the book.

The app includes an optional passage translation feature that, if used, sends a short excerpt of text to a third-party translation service (MyMemory or a configured DeepL endpoint). This feature is entirely opt-in and is not used for any purpose other than translating the selected passage at your request. No personal data is transmitted.

The app also includes an optional Share feature that opens WhatsApp or your device's native share sheet. This is only triggered when you explicitly tap Share, and no data is sent without your action.

Third-Party Services

When the optional translation feature is used, text excerpts may be sent to MyMemory (a free public translation API). Please refer to their privacy policy for details on how they handle submitted text. No user identifiers are sent alongside translation requests.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No account creation or personal information is required to use the app.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in future, the updated version will be made available at this URL. The effective date will be updated accordingly.
